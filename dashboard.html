Trust & Significance Are Inversely Linked

In 403 Cochrane meta-analyses, trust score (robustness × quality) and statistical significance (|z|) share a Frank copula with θ = -2.676 (Kendall’s τ = -0.279, p < 0.001), confirming symmetric negative dependence with no tail concentration.
